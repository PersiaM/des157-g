chapter one

The journey

Spring 2009,

Tehran, Iran

We Were Hopeful...

The election was few days away and the country was in a state of excitement. It acted as a promisisng tool for a better future in people's minds, especially younger generation. We were extremely excited to vote for the reformist candidate who promised change and a bright future especially for the young. The street were filled with positivity and hope.Hope for change...

I found my sister after few hours and we were both safe but horrified. We all were in disbelief about the events we saw that day...

On the plane I kept thinking about all the events that happened, about my family and friends, and about starting a new life abroad...
